Gusesengura ibisubizo ni ingingo igaragara mu buyobozi bw'ingengo z'igikorwa. Gusesengura ibisubizo neza bishobora gufasha amatsinda kugabanya ibyago no kongera ubwiza bw'ibyemezo mu bihe bikomeye. Dore gahunda zitanu zikoreshwa cyane zo gusesengura ibisubizo:

## 1. Uburyo Bwo Gukora Ibyemezo Bushingiye ku Mibare

Kubaka ingingo zo gusesengura ibisubizo zishingiye ku mibare kugirango wizere ko buri cyemezo gifite inyungu z'amakuru zemewe...

## 2. Kwereka Mubare Runaka

Guhindura imibare ikomeye ibe amakuru abonwa neza ukoresheje amashusho, urubuga rw'amakuru, n'ibindi bikoresho byo kwereka...

## 3. Gusesengura Ibisubizo mu Bwoko Bw'Imbonerahamwe

Gukora imikorere y'ibyemezo mu bihe by'urubuga kugirango wibuze ibisubizo bishoboka ku nzira zitandukanye z'ibyemezo...
