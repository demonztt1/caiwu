## Ibyerekeye Serivisi

### Kuki Kongera Ubwenge mu Buryo Bw'ingano Bikenewe?

Muri iki gihe cyihutirwa, kumva neza no kongera ubwenge mu nzira z'ubumenyi ni ingenzi mu gushaka akazi. Kongera ubwenge mu mwuga gakondo rimaze igihe bishingiye ku bintu byihariye, ariko uko dusuzumye tungana bigatuma uko ukura **ushobora gupimwa, gusubiramo, no kugereranywa**.

 - Agaciro k'ingenzi ka Serivisi

**Kwiyumvira Gushingiye ku Birebana"
- Gusuzuma neza ubushobozi bwawe muri bidukikije by'ikoranabuhanga, ubucuruzi, n'ubuyobozi ukoresheje uko dusuzumye dukora"
- Menya ubushobozi bwawe n'ibibazo byo kongera ubwenge"
- Tanga impuzandengo yo kugereranya n'abandi bafite ubuhanga"

**Inzira Iri K'umugaragaro yo Kongera Ubwenge"
- Kora gahunda zihariye zo kongera ubwenge zishingiye ku mimerere yawe n'ibyifuzo"
- Sobanura ubushobozi ukeneye kwiga n'ibipimo ukeneye kugeraho muri buri gice"
- Tanga ibimenyetso by'ingano by'ibyagezweho"

**Gushigikira Kongera Ubwenge"
- Ukuboza amezi kurikira gahunda neza"
- Guhindura uko ukora ubukungu bishingiye ku mimerere"
- Tanga inama n'ubuhanga bwo kongera ubwenge"

**Abagomba"
- "Abatangira Akazi": Bashaka kwihutira gukora akazi no kwirinda kongera ubwenge utabizi"
- "Abahindura Akazi": Bakeneye gusubiramo uko bakora akazi no gushaka inzira nshya zo kongera ubwenge"
- "Abayobozi bo Hagati": Bashaka kongera ubushobozi bwose mu buryo bukurikije gukora no kwitegura kuzamuka"
- "Abahanga mu Bikorabuhanga": Bashaka kongerera ubucuruzi n'ubuyobozi ubushobozi bwo kongera ubwenge bwose"

**Isezerano rya Serivisi"

Dusezeranya kugufasha muri amezi 3 yo gukorera:
1. Kugira ishusho y'ubushobozi bwawe"
2. Kora inzira yo kongera ubwenge"
3. Gera ku kongera ubwenge bupimike"
4. Gira ibikoresho n'uburyo bwo kongera ubwenge"

**Tangira uko ukura mu buryo bw'ingano nonaha, ukore ko umuhate wawe uba ufite inzira n'igisubizo!"
