## Ayɛ ho nsɛm

### Nsusuwii awerɛhow ne nnoɔma a wɔde bɛyɛ

Wɔ akontabuo adwumayɛ a ɛyɛ den mu no, nsusuwii no hyia nsɛnnennen a ɛfa nsɛm a enni mu, nsesae pii, ne nsoɛe a wonnim. Yɛn nsusuwii nhwehwɛmu ne nhweɛso ayɛ no sesa wo nsusuwii kwan no fi "adwenem" kɔ "akontabuo so".

### Nnoɔma a ɛho hia a wɔde di dwuma

**Bea Mfoni Nnoɔma a Wɔde Di Dwuma**
- Sesa nsusuwii a ɛyɛ den no ayɛ bea a wubetumi ayɛ no ho adwene
- Yɛ nsesae a ɛba nsusuwii akwan ahorow so
- Hwehwɛ awerɛhow ne nnoɔma a wonya fi nnoɔma ahorow so

**Nhweɛso a Esesa Nnoɔma**
- Kyerɛ nsusuwii mfoni nsesae bere nyinaa
- Kyerɛ nkitahodi a ɛda nnoɔma a ɛho hia ntam
- Bo ma wosisi nnoɔma na wohu nsoɛe ansa na eba

**Adwene Nkyerɛkyerɛ Nnoɔma a Wɔde Di Dwuma**
- Hu awerɛhow kyerɛwde wɔ nsusuwii mu otomatik
- Ma awerɛhow nkyerɛkyerɛ a ebetumi aba
- Kae nnoɔma a wɔde bɛsi awerɛhow so

### Nnoɔma a Wɔde Di Dwuma

- **Nsusuwii a Wɔde Di Dwuma**: Nsusuwii akɛse te sɛ aduru foforo a wɔde bɛba, akontabuo mu nkɔso, adwumayɛ nsesae
- **Sika a Wɔde Bɔ Ho Mfoni**: Sika a wɔde bɔ nnoɔma ho mfoni, nsusuwii a wɔde bɔ nnoɔma bom, nnoɔma a wɔde bɛyɛ ayɛ
- **Nnoɔma a Wɔde Yɛ Adwuma**: Nnoɔma a wɔde yɛ adwuma no ayɛ, nnoɔma a ɛyɛ, sika a wɔde bɛyɛ adwuma
- **Awerɛhow Siesie**: Awerɛhow a wohu, nhwehwɛmu, nkyerɛkyerɛ, ne nnoɔma a wɔde bɛyɛ

### Nnoɔma a Wɔde Ma

1. **Nsusuwii Nhweɛso Mfoni**: Nsusuwii boafo nnoɔma a wode di dwuma
2. **Nsusuwii Nhwehwɛmu Nkyerɛwde**: Ahotoso nhwehwɛmu a ɛkɔ akyiri ne nnoɔma a wɔde bɛyɛ
3. **Kuayɛ Nwomasua**: Nsusuwii nnoɔma a wɔde di dwuma ne nnoɔma a ɛyɛ
4. **Nnoɔma a Wɔde Yɛ Adwuma Da**: Sesa nsusuwii mfoni wɔ nnoɔma a wonya fi nnoɔma a wɔde di dwuma so

**Ma nsusuwii a ɛho hia nyinaa nyɛ akontabuo a wɔde bɔ ho ban, ma nnoɔma a wonnim no nyɛ den ma wɔahu na wɔayɛ!"
