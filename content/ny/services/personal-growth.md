## Zambiri Za Utumiki

### Chifukwa Chiyani Kukula Kwa Kuwantitative?

M'zaka zosintha mwamsanga, kudziwa bwino ndi njira zaukadaulo za kukula ndi zofunika kwambiri pa kutsogolera kwa ntchito. Kukula kwa ntchito kwa kale kumayenderana ndi maganizo okhaokha, pomwe kudziwona kwathu kwa kuwantitative kumapanga njira yanu ya kukula kukhala **yoyezeka, yokonzekera, ndi yodziwika bwino**.

### Mtengo Waukulu Wa Utumiki

**Kudziwona Pokhazikika Pa Zambiri**
- Kudziwona kwa thanzi m'magawo atatu a sayansi, bizinesi, ndi kasamalidwe pogwiritsa ntchito mamodeli oyesera a sayansi
- Kudziwa thanzi lanu lalikulu ndi zopanda thanzi
- Kupereka zoyerezera ndi ena ophunzira bwino

**Njira Yodziwika Bwino Ya Kukula**
- Kupanga mapangidwe oti akwaniritse ndi kugwira ntchito okhazikika pa momwe mwalili ndi zolinga zanu
- Kufotokozera thanzi lofunika lophunzira ndi mayeso ofunikira pa gawo lililonse
- Kupereka zizindikiro zoyezeka za kuyenda

**Thandizo Losalekeza La Kukula**
- Kutsatira pamwezi kumatsimikizira kugwira ntchito kwa dongosolo
- Kusintha mwamsanga kwa njira zokhazikika pogwiritsa ntchito momwe zilili
- Kupereka malangizo a ukadaulo okula ndi malangizo a zida

**Ongogwiritsa Ntchito**
- **Oyambira Ntchito**: Akufuna kumanga mwachangu chitukuko cha ntchito ndi kupewa kukula mosadziwa
- **Osintha Ntchito**: Akufuna kukonza njira yatsopano ya ntchito ndi kufuna njira zatsopano zokhazikika
- **Asamalira Apakatikati**: Akufuna kukweza thanzi lathunthu mwa dongosolo ndi kukonzekera pa udindo wapamwamba
- **Akatswiri A Sayansi**: Akufuna kukulitsa thanzi la bizinesi ndi kasamalidwe pa kukula kwathunthu

**Chitsimikizo Cha Utumiki**

Timatsimikizira kuti m'miyezi 3 ya utumiki, tidzakuthandizani:
1. Kumanga chithunzi chodziwika bwino cha thanzi lanu
2. Kupanga njira yaukadaulo ya kukula
3. Kufikira kukweza koyezeka kwa thanzi
4. Kugwiritsa ntchito zida ndi njira zokhazikika zokula

**Yambitsani ulendo wanu wokula wokhazikika tsopano, pochita kuti chilichonse chifukwe chikhale ndi malangizo odziwika bwino ndi malipiro!**
