## Zambiri Zaumwini

### Mavuto Osankhira Ndithandizo

M'malo ovuta abizinesi, zisankho nthawi zambiri zimakumana ndi mavuto opanda uthenga wathunthu, kusinthasintha kwambiri, ndi zotsatira zosadziwika. Umwini wathu wokafukufuku zosankha ndikuwonetsa umasinthira njira yanu yosankha kuchokera "kuganiza" kupita "kugwiritsa ntchito deta".

### Ubwino Wateknoloji

**Tekinoloje Yoyeserera Malo**
- Sinthani mavuto ovuta osankhira kukhala malo oyeserera
- Yererani zotsatira zosiyana zosankha
- Yezani chiwopsezo ndi zobwerera za njira zosiyana

"Kuoneka Koyeserera"
- Onetsani kusintha kwamelodi yosankha nthawi yomweyo
- Kuonetsa magwirizano azindikiro zofunika
- Thandizani kusintha ndikuonanso zotsatira

"Dongosolo Lochenjeza"
- Dziwani zizindikiro zachiwopsezo mosankha
- Pereka chenjezo zachiwopsezo
- Lungirani njira zotetezera

### Momwe Mungagwiritse Ntchito

- "Konzekera Bizinesi": Zisankho zofunika monga kumasulidwa kwa katundu watsopano, kulowa pamisika, kusintha kwabizinesi
- "Kuyeza Kugula": Kugula mapulojekiti, kusankha kugwirizana, kukonza kugawa zinthu
- "Kukonza Ntchito": Kubwezeretsa njira, kuwonjezera ntchito, kudziteteza mtengo
- "Kusamala Ngozi": Kudziwa, kuyeza, kuchenjeza, ndi kuyankha ngozi

### Zoperekedwa

1. "Modeli Yowoneka Yosankha": Chida chothandiza posankha
2. "Reporti Yokafukufuku Yosankha": Kafukufuku wathunthu wamomwe zingachitike ndi malangizo
3. "Kuphunzitsa Gulu": Njira zogwiritsira ntchito zida zosankha ndi njira zabwino
4. "Kukonza Ntchito": Kusintha mamelodi osankha pogwiritsa ntchito deta

"Pangani chilichonse chofunika chosankha chogwiritsa ntchito deta, pititsani patsogolo zosadziwika!"
