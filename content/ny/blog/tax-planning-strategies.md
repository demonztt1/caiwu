Kowerengera zochita ndi gawo lofunika la kampani yowerengera njira. Kowerengera zochita molondola kungathandize makampani kuchepetsa zoopsa ndi kupititsa patsogo kwaluso kochita zochita m'malo zosavuta. Pali njira zisanu zowerengera zochita:

## 1. Njira Zowerengera Zochita Zogwiritsa Ntchito Zinthu

Kumanga njira zowerengera zochita zogwiritsa ntchito zinthu kuti tatsimikizire kuti chilichonse chochita chimagwiritsa ntchito zinthu zokhulupirika...

## 2. Kuwonetsa Makanema Osiyanasiyana

Kusintha zinthu zosavuta kukhala zithunzi zoweruza popita pa machati, mabodi oyesera, ndi zida zina zowerengera...

## 3. Kuwunika Kwakukonzedwe Kwa Malo

Kupanga zitsanzo zowerengera zochita m'malo opanga kuti apafanizire zotsatira zingathekere munjira zosiyanasiyana zochita...
