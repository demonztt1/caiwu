## 1. Data-Inotungamira Sarudzo Mhando

## 2. Multi-dimensional Visualization Kuratidzwa

Kushandura data yakaoma kuita ruzivo rwekuona kunonzwisisika kuburikidza nemachati, madhibhodhi, uye zvimwe zvekushandisa zvekuratidza...
