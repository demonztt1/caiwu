Firlitug nguur gu am solo la ci njàngum njiitu mboolaay. Firlitug nguur gu jub di wallu mboolaay yi dàq metit yi di yokku saytu nguur gi ci jawwu yu yàgg. Yiléene juroom benn jikkoomi yu am solo ci firlitug nguur:

## 1. Nataayu Nguur Yuy Jëm ci Xibaar

Tabax nataayu firlitug nguur yuy jëm ci xibaar ngir wone ne nguur képp amna jàll ci xibaar yu nga yàgg...

## 2. Xëytaago yu Bari Fanaan

Soppi xibaar yu yàgg ci xibaar yu gis yu néew ci jàmm biy jëfe ci carte, tableau de bord, ak yeneen jumtukaayu xëytaago...

## 3. Xeetu Jumtukaayu Dottub Nguur

Firlitu ci jawwu yu xel ci anamu gis-gis yu nguur ngir seetlu ci benn lu am ci yooni nguur yu bari...
