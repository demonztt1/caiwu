Ŋkeke ƒe nyaŋkeke nye nu si le vevie le asitsɛa ƒe dɔwɔƒea. Ŋkeke ƒe nyaŋkeke si sɔ gɔme la ate ŋu aɖa ƒe habɔbɔawo ɖe dzɔdzɔe ƒe gomekpɔkpɔ ŋu eye wɔaɖe ŋkeke ƒe nyateƒe ɖe dzɔdzɔe me. Afisia wo atɔ̃ ƒe ŋkuɖeɖe siwo wozã nya ŋkeke:

## 1. Data ƒe Dɔwɔƒe Ŋkeke ƒe Dɔwɔƒewo

Dze data ƒe dɔwɔƒe ŋkeke ƒe nyaŋkeke ƒe dɔwɔƒea ɖo esime wòdi be ŋkeke sia ŋkeke le data ƒe dɔwɔwɔ aɖe si wotsɔa ɖe eŋu ene...

## 2. Vevea Gomekpɔkpɔ ƒe Kpɔɖeŋu

Trɔ data siwo le vevie ɖe kpɔɖeŋu ƒe nyatakaka siwo wòaŋu ɖe chartwo, dashboard, kple kpɔɖeŋu ƒe dɔwɔƒe bubuwo gbɔ...

## 3. State Space Simulation ƒe Nyaŋkeke

Ɖe ŋkeke ƒe dɔwɔƒea ɖe virtual teƒe me ɖe ŋkeke ƒe mɔ siwo ɖe mɔ teɖe teɖe la ƒe nuwɔna siwo ate ŋu adzɔ ene...
