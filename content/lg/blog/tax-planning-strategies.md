Okunonyereza ku nteekateeka kye kimu ku bintu eby'okumuko mu kukwasisa ebitongole. Okunonyereza ku nteekateeka okulungi kuyinza okuyamba ebitongole okukendeeza obuzibu n'okwongera ekibalago ky'enkola mu nsi ennyingo. Zino z'enkola ettaano ez'okunonyereza ku nteekateeka:

## 1. Enkola Z'okweyambisa Amakulu

Okuzimba enkola z'okunonyereza ku nteekateeka ezikwasisa amakulu okusobola okukakasa nti buli nteekateeka erina amakulu agakwasizibwa...

## 2. Okulabikisa mu Nateegyeko

Okugyusa amakulu amanyi n'okugayusa mu bifaananyi ebirabika olina okuyambisa ebifaananyi, dashiboodi, n'ebirala eby'okulabikisa...

## 3. Okwekennenya mu Nateegyeko

Okwekennenya enkola z'enteekateeka mu nsi ezikwasisibwa okusobola okulaga ebiva mu nteekateeka ez'enjawulo...
