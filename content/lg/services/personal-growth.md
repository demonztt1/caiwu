## Ebikwata ku Muliwo

### Lwaki Okukula Omuntu mu Namba Kyetagisa?

Mu kiseera kino ekyakyusaganya ennyo, okwekenenya obwannimiro n'enkola z'okukula eza sayansi kye kisumuluzo ku lw'okukola omulimu. Okukula kw'emirimu eky'edda kyesigamye ku kugera ku nkola, naye okunonyereza kwaffe okw'enkula mu namba kukukola enkola yo okukula **okupimika, okukolagana, era n'okubaawo**.

 - Akakwate ku Muliwo

**Okwekenenya Okwesigamye ku Namba"
- Okunonyereza ku nkola zo omye mu nkola z'emikono, eby'ebibuga, n'okulabirira nga okozesa enkola z'okunonyereza z'okusunsula"
- Laga amaanyi go ogisinga obunyi n'ebifo by'okukula"
- Waayo okugeraageranya n'abalala ab'amaanyi"

**Enkola Eggyemu y'Okukula"
- Kola enkola z'okukula ezigyemu n'ezikola nga zitegeereddwa ku nkola zo omye n'ebigendererwa byo"
- Laga enkola ezetaagisa okwega n'emikono egya okutuukirizibwa mu buli kiseera"
- Waayo obubonero obupimika bwa buli kkubo"

**Okuyamba Okukula Okweyongera"
- Okugoberera omwezi kitegeera nti enkola ekola bulungi"
- Okuyiiyiza kw'amaanyi enkola z'okukula nga zitegeereddwa ku nkola eziriwo"
- Waayo amagezi ag'okukula n'okulaga ebintu ebikozesebwa"

**Abagoberezi"
- "Aboogera ku Mirimu": Bagala okweyongera okukola emirimu nga baleetera okukula okutaliimu kwekenenya"
- "Aboakyusakyusa Emirimu": Betaaga okuddamu okulonda enkola y'emirimu n'okunoonya enkola empya z'okukula"
- "Abakulembeze wakati": Bagala okweyongera enkola ez'omuddiringanwa mu nteekateeka n'okweyamba ku bifo eby'enkizo"
- "Bannamaguzi w'Emikono": Bagala okwanika enkola z'ebibuga n'okulabirira ku lw'okukula okw'omuddiringanwa"

**Endagiriro y'Omuliwo"

Tweesigamye n'okukuyamba mu kiseera ky'ameezi 3 okuyitira mu muluwo:
1. Kola akabonero akaggyemu ak'amaanyi go
2. Kola enkola y'okukula eya sayansi
3. Tuuke ku nkula y'amaanyi epimika
4. Funayo ebikozesebwa n'enkola z'okukula okweyongera

**Tandika olugendo lwo lw'okukula mu namba kati, kola nti buli kkolakola lina nkola eggyemu n'empeera!"
