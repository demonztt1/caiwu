Ƴeewtere ko haɗaande mawnde e fijirde laawol pinal. Ƴeewtere mo ƴamɗum wallata ɗiɗi ceeruɗi ɓeydude huunde e ɓeydude ndeenka ceeragol ɗin laawol cee-cure. Ɗee ɗoo ceɓɓe jowi ɓurɗe nafa e Ƴeewtere:

## 1. Model Ceeragol Jowitiiɗum e Keɓe

Mawninde model Ƴeewtere jowitiiɗum e keɓe ngel saafaa kala ceeragol ina woodi ballal keɓe huunde...

## 2. Hollire Cuɓal Muudɗal

Waylu keɓe ɗin ɓurɗe cuuɓal hollire ndiyam ɗin hollirɗe e jaajol, dashboard, e ɓesngu hollire goɗɗe...

## 3. Ƴeewtere Doolal Laawol Ngonka

Doolal ceeragol ɗin laawol virtual ngel fiyaku laawol ceeragol ɗin seeɗa...
