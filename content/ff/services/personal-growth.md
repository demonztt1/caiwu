## Cifooji Service

### Ko haɗa Caggal Nguurndam Yiyande?

E ngun ndeen yaɓɓitiiɗe, anndal hoore e laawol caggal ganndal ko caɗeele ngam yeewte gollal. Caggal gollal adunaawi ina waɗa e tiiɗno hakkillo, to fijirde amen yiyande ina waɗa nguurndam maa **yiytondir, feewdindir e faydindir**.

### Nguurndam Service Mawngal

**Anndal Hoore Nguurndam Datal**
- Fijirde goonga nguurndam maa jooni e taƴƴere teknikal, ganndal e darnde e model fijirde ganndal
- Ƴeewto gaawo maa mawngal e cakkitte caggal
- Jokkondir e gaawo worɓe ɓurɓe nguurndam

**Laawol Caggal Feeñungal**
- Mawu naawre caggal goonga e golle ɗe ngam nguurndam maa jooni e foolde maa
- Ƴeewto ɓesngu inaɓe e ɓamtudi ina heɓoto e kala yontere
- Hokku maanduɗe yiyande ɓesngu

**Ballal Caggal Jokkinde**
- Dirtogol kala lewru ina seeda golle naawre nden
- Waylude laawol caggal e ɗemngal nguurndam jooni
- Hokku tawa e celji baawɗe ngam caggal

### Worɓe Nguuraaɓe

- **Fuɗɗiiɓe Gollal**: Njiɗ-ɗi tawu gaawo gollal aawɗi e daɗdu caggal bonɗo
- **Worɓe Waylude Gollal**: Njiɗ-ɗi heɓ laawol gollal kese e yiylo laawol caggal kese
- **Darɓe Lammbɗe**: Njiɗ-ɗi ɓeyditu nguurndam fotde e wallitde darnde mawnude
- **Ganɓe Teknikal**: Njiɗ-ɗi ɓeyditu gollal ganndal e darnde ngam caggal fotde

### Jippagol Service

Min ndippiiɗi wallude maa e lewru 3 service nden:
1. Tawu profil nguurndam hooreejo feeñungal
2. Mawu laawol caggal nguurndam ganndal
3. Heɓ ɓeyditgol nguurndam yiyande
4. Heɓ koɗe e laɓɓe ngam caggal jokkinde

**Fuɗɗo nguurndam maa yiyande jooni, waɗdu kala booɗungal ina woodi laawol e nafoje!**
